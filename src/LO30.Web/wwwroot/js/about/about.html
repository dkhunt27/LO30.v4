Livonia Over 30 Hockey ANY DAY PLAYING HOCKEY IS A GREAT DAY!

A league built around fun, fair, and competitive play for all ages (over 30) and skill levels.

Why Livonia Over 30?

If you want to play hockey, you live in Livonia, and you are over 30, then you need to get into the Livonia Over 30 Hockey league. The league has been in existence since 1970. It is loaded with tradition, families, and fun. Through a unique rating system, draft process, and line structure, the league makes it fair and competitive for all ages (over 30) and skill levels. The facilities are great, the ice times are fantastic, and the cost is just right!

Join the League (Or Sub in it)

Interested in playing in the Best hockey league in Michigan? You must meet the criteria below to join the league. If you can't meet all the criteria but still want to play, you can possibly sub in the league. Currently there is a wait list to join, so sign up ASAP!

  • Livonia Resident
  • Over 30 years old
  • Commit to playing hockey Thursday and Sunday nights

To get your name added to the wait list and/or sub list, send an email to information@livoniaover30hockey.com with the following information:

  • EmailTo: information@livoniaover30hockey.com
  • Subject: I want to join the league! OR I want to sub in the league!
  • Full Name
  • Full Address (Street, City, State, Zip)
  • Phone Number(s) (home, work, cell - indicate best contact method/time)
  • Name(s) of anybody currently playing in the league that knows you
  • Brief background of your hockey playing abilities (years played, where you last played, position(s), approx. how good a player you are)

Sponsorships

If you are interested in sponsoring a team in league, please send an email to information@livoniaover30hockey.com Include your full name, contact phone number, and best time to reach you. You do not need to play hockey, although many of our sponsors are also league members.
